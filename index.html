Generative AI for Aerial Image Analysis
Master's thesis. Evaluated open-source VLMs (Qwen-2.5-VL-7B) via zero-shot & few-shot prompting to classify attic usability across 745 annotated aerial building images, achieving up to 69% accuracy. Built a multimodal inference pipeline linking imagery to PostgreSQL geospatial records. Benchmarked 6 VLMs and analysed XAI failure modes including hallucination and spatial misalignment.

GenReq — AI for Requirements Engineering
Co-led architecture of a full-stack AI tool reducing SRS generation from days to 20–30 min at ~$0.60/session. Built a RAG pipeline with LangChain, ChromaDB, and FastAPI for auto-generation of requirements specs, UML diagrams, and LaTeX-compiled PDF reports. Led architecture evolution from Streamlit/SQLite to a React + MongoDB production stack.

XAI for Graph Neural Networks
Analysed the heterogeneous MovieLens100K graph (943 users, 1,682 movies, 80K edges) in PyTorch Geometric. Applied Captum Integrated Gradients to explain a SAGEConv-based GNN via link prediction. Ran ablation experiments — feature-zeroing and timing-edge removal — to validate XAI behaviour on categorical graph data.
