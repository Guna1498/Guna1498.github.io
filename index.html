About the Explorer

I'm a M.Sc. Computer Science graduate from the University of Paderborn with 2+ years of hands-on research at Fraunhofer IEE. My work sits at the crossroads of machine intelligence and real-world data.

For my Master's thesis, I dove into the frontier of Vision Language Models — evaluating how open-source VLMs can classify urban infrastructure from aerial imagery, linking pixels to geospatial databases to answer real planning questions.

I believe the best engineers are perpetual explorers — always asking why, always building towards what's next.
